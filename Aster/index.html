Bone Marrow Trasplant(BMT) better known as haematopoietic stem cell Trasplant(HSCT) as bone marrow cells are transplanted

Repalcement of Diseased or defective marrow with marrow from a healthy donor

Medicines and in certain situations radiotherapy as well is given to get rid of diseased marrow and bone marrow cells from healthy donor is given to replace it.

In autologus bone marrow traplant, high dose chemotheropy is given to kill cancer cells and child own marrow cells are given back.
